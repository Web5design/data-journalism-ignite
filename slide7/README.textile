h1. More basic Excel

This slide shows situations in which data or data analysis was explicitly referenced in stories that won the Pulitzer Prize for investigative journalism between 1985 and 2012.

h2. The data

The data was gathered by hand from the "Pulitzer website":http://www.pulitzer.org and other research. The critiera for determining whether data was instrumental to the story was whether it was referenced directly in the text of submitted stories.

h3. The visualization

The visualization was inspired by a "similar technique":http://rainmaker.apps.cironline.org/donors/stephen-l-bing/ we used at the Center for Investigative Reporting to outline trends in political campaign giving by wealthy donors.
